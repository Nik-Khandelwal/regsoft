INSTRUCTIONS

  • Instruction 1 : Go to BOSM 2018. Register your college by filling in necessary data.

  • Instruction 3 : You can register yourself as a captain, participant or coach.
  • Instruction 4 : You can only register for one sport as a captain. And for others you may register as an participant
  • Instruction 5 : You can register multiple sports in case you are only a participant.
  • Instruction 6 : You can only register in one sport if you are a Coach.
  • Instruction 7 : Registering your college does not confirm participation.
  • Instruction 8 : After registering, confirm your email by clicking on activation link mailed to you to your registered email address and wait further correspondence.
  • Instruction 9 : We will then contact you to identify a group leader (GL) among the people registered from your college and will represent your team in campus.
  • Instruction 10 : The GL will be confirmed and an activation mail will be sent. The GL will be able to login and register all the participants, captain and coach.
  • Instruction 11 : Registration of any participant, captain or coach does not confirm participation.
  • Instruction 12 : Participants with same mobile number and gender will be considered as single participant.
  • Instruction 13 : All participants will receive emails with their credentials at their registered email address.
  • Instruction 14 : Captains can login as soon as they have been registered by GL and can register their teams.
  • Instruction 15 : Participants and coaches can login only after their team has been confirmed.
  • Instruction 16 : Confirmation mails with the names of confirmed participaticipants will be sent to the registered email addresses of the GL and the captain of the team which has been confirmed. In case there is no captain registered in the team, Confirmation mails will be sent to the registered email addresses of the GL and all the participants in the team.
  • Instruction 17 : The shortlisted participants will be required to pay the participation fee. The payment details will be available at the time of payment.

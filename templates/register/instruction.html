Adding New Participants When you first open the page you won't have any participants in your college added. To add a Participant, click on the "Add Participant" button in the left hand side near the top (marked using Red Border).
Adding Details/Submit After clicking on it. You are greeted with a form at the bottom of the page. Please fill in the details of the Paricipant you would like to register. Click "Submit Participants" to add them to your list. If you would like to add more than one participants at a time click "Add More".
Editing Details If you made a mistake in adding someone or misclicked to add more participants just delete the extra participant using the remove button beside it (marked in Green).
Selecting Sport The participants list in the left table are all disabled meaning you can't add them yet until you select a sport. To select one click on the select sport button on the right hand side towards the top (marked in Green).
Selecting Sport to Add Select the sport that you would like to add the participants to from the list of options and click on it (in this case I selected CRICKET (BOYS) marked in Blue).
Adding Participants to Sport After selecting a sport the participants on the left side get enabled to be added. But you can see there are some participants that didn't get activated. This is because either the Participant is a Coach in one sport (in which case the participant cannot participate in any other sport) or the participant is already registered in the selected sport. Click on the green add button beside a participant to add them to the right side and disabling them on the left side.
Adding Captain/Coach A participant when added to the right side table is added by default as a participant in that Sport. If you would like to make a specific person as a Captain/Coach. Click on the corresponding checkbox to toggle between making the participant a Captain/Coach.
Confirming Participation You can also remove a participant from the right side table if you added someone by mistake before submitting. After you have added the participants to the right side table and have selected the appropriate captain/coach click on Submit to confirm their participation in the selected sport. Please wait while we save your preferences.
Upload Documents After your team is confirmed for Participation by PCR you can upload your documents by clicking on "Doc Upload" near the top right (marked in Green).
